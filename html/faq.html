Soft Crash Games

  • Why do your demos on your Games page have no graphics?

    • As an organization soft crash does not have artists, we create tools
    so that those in our parent company can give them to artists to create tools.
    So our tech demo's are more proof of concept since we don't employ any artists.

  • Why do you have no major releases like any other game studio?

    • As mentioned in question 1 we were never meant to get a following, so we
    have only made systems for others to implement.

  • I want to support you, how can I?

    • Buy games that use our technology, so really any RPGs made by EA, so we can keep
    funding our tool creation.

  • So are you ever going to release a game?

    • A year ago we would have said no, but out engineers have spent so much time
    developing these tools that we feel we're ready to transition into a small team.
    Our Games page has a small demo on the dialogue system we have developed for the now named
    Bartender 2088. Other systems are in development, and we have the blessing of our parent company, so wish us luck as we begin 2013!
